🖋 The Importance of Typing

In today’s fast-moving digital world, typing is not just a skill — it’s a necessity. From sending emails to preparing official documents, every profession demands speed and accuracy on the keyboard. Good typing skills save time, improve productivity, and open doors to better job opportunities.

Whether you are a student preparing for competitive exams, a professional handling office tasks, or a freelancer working online, typing efficiently gives you an edge. It’s not just about pressing keys — it’s about communicating effectively in the digital age.
